Basketball

Home / basketball


Assignment: Recreate an arcade game using the electronics provided. The game must use the Internet of Things.

My first course after transferring to Tufts was "Engineering Sciences 93: Internet of Things". This course was taught remotely (due to the pandemic) during the summer of 2020, and each student worked with Arduinos to integrate hardware, online databases, and even augmented reality (using Vuforia). My final project involved using the materials I had in home to recreate a miniaturized version of the classic basketball arcade game with a live digital leaderboard.

I documented all of my work for that class on this Google Site.
